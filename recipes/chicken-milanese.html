Chicken Milanese

Description

Chicken Milanese is a classic Italian dish featuring tender chicken cutlets coated in breadcrumbs, pan-fried until crispy, and served with a refreshing arugula salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts, butterflied and pounded thin
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Extra virgin olive oil, for salad

Steps

  1. Season the chicken cutlets with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Set up a breading station with three shallow bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan cheese.
  3. Dredge each chicken cutlet in flour, shaking off excess. Dip it into the eggs, then coat it with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing lightly to adhere.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add the breaded chicken cutlets and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Transfer the chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.
  7. In a bowl, toss the arugula with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  8. Serve the chicken cutlets with the arugula salad and lemon wedges on the side.
