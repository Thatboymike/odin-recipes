Sunday Traditional Meatballs

Description

These classic Italian meatballs are tender, juicy, and simmered in a rich tomato sauce, making them the perfect comfort food for your Sunday gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb (225g) ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic, parsley, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into meatballs, about 1.5 inches in diameter.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  5. Pour marinara sauce over the meatballs and reduce heat to low.
  6. Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through.
  7. Garnish with additional parsley and serve warm with pasta, bread, or as desired.
